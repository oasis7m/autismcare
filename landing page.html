Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rehabilitation platform

Have More Questions?

Our support team is ready to assist you with any additional inquiries you may have about our platform.

Who can benefit from these rehabilitation games?

Our games are specifically designed for children on the autism spectrum between the ages of 3-12. However, older children with similar therapeutic needs may also benefit from our platform.

What equipment do I need to use the platform?

You only need a device with a camera (smartphone, tablet, or computer) and internet access. Our platform is web-based and doesn't require any specialized hardware.

How does the emotion recognition technology work?

Using computer vision algorithms, our system analyzes facial expressions and non-verbal cues to identify emotions in real-time. This information helps personalize the experience and provides valuable insights for caregivers.

Is parental involvement required during sessions?

While children can engage independently with many activities, we recommend caregiver participation, especially early on. Our platform provides real-time suggestions and progress tracking to facilitate meaningful engagement.

Can I track my child's progress over time?

Yes, our platform generates detailed progress reports showing improvements in attention span, emotional recognition, communication skills, and specific developmental milestones. These can be shared with therapists or educators.
